Our Services

Inbound Customer Service

Inventum Strategies delivers a full-service, scalable customer service operation that integrates seamlessly with your existing business workflows. Whether you need 24/7 answering, overflow capacity during peaks, appointment scheduling, dispatch routing, or escalation handling, our trained team acts as your front-line support.

Core Capabilities:
  • 24/7 Live Answering: Always-on availability so no call goes unanswered, including nights, weekends, and holidays.
  • Overflow & Peak Support: Temporarily scale your coverage during promotions, product launches, or seasonal spikes without hiring.
  • Appointment Scheduling & Reminders: Manage bookings, confirmations, and automated reminders to reduce no-shows.
  • Dispatch Coordination: Route service calls based on geography, urgency, and technician availability with robust escalation control.
  • Multichannel Handling: Phone, email, and chat support with unified context across channels for improved customer experience.
  • Escalation Management: Built-in SOPs for intelligent escalation to your internal teams or partners.

We tailor processes to your vertical—e-commerce, logistics, home services, SaaS, and more—while delivering branded, high-quality interactions.

Lead Generation & Sales Support

Our offshore sales development resources work as a seamless extension of your business development team to generate pipeline and accelerate conversion. From cold outreach to lead qualification and demo booking, Inventum Strategies creates predictable lead flow tailored to your buyer persona.

Core Offerings:
  • Outbound Prospecting: Targeted campaigns using scripted cold calls, emails, and follow-ups to generate interest.
  • Lead Qualification: Validate fit, budget, authority, timing with custom frameworks before passing to closers.
  • Warm Transfers: Real-time connection of qualified prospects to your internal sales reps or agents.
  • Appointment Setting: Book demos or discovery calls with high-intent leads, reducing friction in the sales cycle.
  • Pipeline Nurturing: Multi-touch follow-up sequences for leads not yet sales-ready, keeping them engaged.
  • Custom Reporting: Conversion metrics, response times, lead scoring, and feedback loops to tune campaigns.

Engagement Models

We offer flexible pricing and engagement structures depending on your needs:

  • Retainer: Fixed monthly capacity for consistent support/lead flow.
  • Performance-Based: Pay per qualified lead, appointment, or transfer.
  • Hybrid: Base retainer plus bonuses for volume or conversion thresholds.
