Industries We Serve

Inventum Strategies has deep experience supporting businesses across a variety of verticals where customer contact volume and sales pipeline quality are critical. We customize our operations to each industry’s unique pain points and buyer behaviors.

E-commerce / DTC

High order volume, frequent returns, and customer inquiries make responsive support essential. We handle order tracking, returns processing, live chat, and lead generation for product upsells, improving customer retention and lifetime value.

Logistics & Freight

Real-time dispatch coordination and client communications are mission critical. Our dispatch routing, after-hours load monitoring, and lead qualification help logistics firms reduce delays and grow shipper relationships.

Property Management

24/7 tenant support, maintenance requests, and leasing inquiries demand a reliable front desk. Our teams field emergency calls, schedule showings, manage tenant onboarding, and qualify prospective renters.

Home Services

Plumbers, HVAC, and restoration companies require fast appointment scheduling and dispatch. We reduce missed calls, book jobs directly, and route emergency service calls to the correct technician.

SaaS & Tech

Support tickets, onboarding questions, and lead qualification for demos are key. We provide tier-1 tech support, user engagement, and SDR-style outreach to move prospects through the funnel.

Subscription & Membership

Recurring revenue models depend on retention. Our customer success touchpoints, renewal outreach, and proactive support keep churn low and lifetime value high.
